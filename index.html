How To Play:

  • Hit spin to run the slot machine.
  • To bet on a row, you must select the checkbox. Each row costs $1.
  • You start with $100. You cannot make bets that you do not have enough money for.
  • There are 6 colors possible, all of which are equally likely
  • Three matching colors in a row pays you the sum of the numbers in the row.
  • If you bet on all three rows, you have the chance to match diagonals, an X pattern, or a blackout.
  • Matching columns DO NOT give a payout.
  • Diagonals pay the sum of the numbers in the diagonal.
  • An X pattern pays $100.
  • A blackout pays $1,000,000.
  • Matching rows still payout when you get diagonals, X's, and blackouts.
